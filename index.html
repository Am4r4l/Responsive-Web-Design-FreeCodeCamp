Chadwick Boseman

a King, a Hero, a Legend.

Here's a timeline of Chadwick's life:

  • 1976 - Born in Anderson, South Carolina
  • 1995 - Graduates from T. L. Hanna High School
  • 1998 - Attended to the summer program of the British American Drama Academy at Balliol College, Oxford, in England.
  • 2000 - Graduates from Howard University in Washington D.C. with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Directing.
  • 2000 - Gets named a Drama League Directing Fellow.
  • 2002 - Becomes a notorious actor and playwrighter performing in multiple productions and winning an AUDELCO award in 2002 for his part in Ron Milner's Urban Transitions.
  • 2003 - Gets casted in his first television role, an episode of Third Watch, and began playing Reggie Montgomery in the daytime soap opera All My Children.
  • 2004 - Comissioned his best know play, Deep Azure which was later nominated for a 2006 Jeff Award for Best New Work.
  • 2008 - Moves to Los Angeles to pursue his film and acting career.
  • 2013 - Plays his breakthrough role in the film 42, in which he portrayed the lead role of baseball legend Jackie Robinson.
  • 2015 - Starts dating singer Taylor Simone Ledward
  • 2016 - Starts portraying the Marvel Comics character T'Challa / Black Panther in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • 2016 - Gets diagnosed with stage III colon cancer.
  • 2018 - Returns as the Black Panther in 2018, in the Black Panther movie, which focused on the character and his home country of Wakanda in Africa. The film opened to great anticipation, becoming one of the highest-grossing films. The role earned Boseman a spot on the 2018 Time 100 as one of the world's most influential people.
  • 2019 - Becomes part of the cast for the Netflix films Da 5 Bloods, directed by Spike Lee, and Ma Rainey's Black Bottom, directed by George C. Wolfe. He took these "bucket-list roles" for opportunities to work with Lee and with Ma Rainey producer Denzel Washington, as well as the opportunity to perform in an August Wilson play, telling Entertainment Weekly that he wanted to make these non-superhero films because "if you don't do the films that you plan to do, I think you wouldn't feel fulfilled as an artist."
  • 2019 - Gets engaged and later married in secret.
  • 2020 - Dies at the age of 43.
